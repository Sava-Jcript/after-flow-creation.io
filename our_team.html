Head designer

Nataliia Kishuk

×

“From times of my childhood, I always dreamt to create beautiful and practical clothes and outfits.” We met Nataliia in May this year and instantly fell in love with her creativity, drive and passion for her work. We included Nataliia in our team as the head tailor and designer. Her unique set of skills, knowledge and more than 10 years of experience in the fashion industry is precious and we are incredibly grateful she likes our initiative and wants to be part of it. Nataliia and her 2-year-old daughter had to leave her home and family in Ukraine due to the war. “The war brought a new reality and now I am here with a new page in my life.” She keeps being positive and we feel privileged to be able to offer her a platform where she can continue realizing her professional dreams.

Tatiana Makhalatko

×

Tatiana was born and raised in Kharkov, Ukraine, which was completely devastated earlier this year by the Russian army, so she decided to leave for Denmark with her daughter. Despite the sad reality, she is very creative, cheerful, and full of energy. Back home, she worked as a fashion designer, tailor and seamstress, and in her free time she really enjoys making beautiful teddy bears for children. We are more than happy to have Tatiana join our team, where she has already designed and sewed some enchanting pieces out of textile waste. As she says herself “creating something new together is always interesting and I believe that it is promising”`
